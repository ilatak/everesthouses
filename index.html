Welcome to Everest Houses!

We have over 20 years of diverse experience in exterior finishing throughout Western Canada. We specialize in handling your entire exterior renovation project or new build. With a wide variety of soffit and fascia materials readily available to us as well as the ability for custom wood working, Everest Houses can offer a wide range of accessories to enhance any project.


We, at Everest Houses also offer custom metal flashings and window capping along with eavestrough and downpipe installation. Furthermore, we have a vast knowledge of building envelope and rain screens so we are able to focus on moisture management and proper air flow in order to prevent unwanted moisture build up and mould growth. Everest Houses will ensure that you choose the right siding, exterior cladding, insulation, and accessories for your project; big or small.
